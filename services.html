ACUPUNCTURE

Acupuncture is a natural, time-tested therapy that supports the body's ability to restore balance and function. By stimulating specific points on the body, acupuncture helps regulate the nervous system, improve circulation, and encourage the body's own healing response. Treatments are personalized to address both physical symptoms and underlying imbalances, supporting long-term relief rather than temporary fixes.

What Acupuncture Can Treat

  • Chronic and acute pain (neck, back, shoulders, hips)
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Stress, anxiety, and nervous system dysregulation
  • Insomnia and poor sleep quality
  • Digestive concerns and bloating
  • Hormonal imbalances and menstrual discomfort
  • Fatigue and low energy
  • Muscle tension and inflammation

CUPPING

Cupping is a therapeutic technique used to release muscle tension, improve circulation, and support the body's natural detoxification process. By creating gentle suction on the skin, cupping helps lift and decompress tight tissues, allowing fresh blood and oxygen to flow more freely. This treatment is especially effective for addressing deep muscular tension and areas of chronic stiffness.

What Cupping Can Treat

  • Muscle tension and tightness
  • Neck, shoulder, and back pain
  • Reduced mobility and stiffness
  • Inflammation and soreness
  • Stress-related tension
  • Post-workout recovery
  • Chronic pain patterns

SEEDING (AURICULOTHERAPY)

Seeding, also known as ear seeding, is a gentle therapy that supports balance and wellness through continuous stimulation of specific points on the ear. Small seeds or beads are placed on targeted areas and secured with adhesive, allowing for ongoing therapeutic support between sessions. This non-invasive treatment is subtle, effective, and ideal for clients seeking sustained benefits without needles.

What Ear Seeding Can Treat

  • Stress and anxiety relief
  • Sleep support and insomnia
  • Hormonal balance
  • Digestive support
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Cravings and appetite regulation
  • Emotional balance and focus
  • Ongoing pain management

ANF THERAPY

ANF (Amino Neuro Frequency) Therapy is a non-invasive treatment that uses small wearable frequency discs to support the body's natural healing and pain-regulation systems. These discs interact with the body's bioelectrical signals, helping reduce inflammation, ease pain, and restore balanced function. ANF Therapy is often combined with acupuncture to enhance results and support longer-lasting relief.

What ANF Therapy Can Treat

  • Acute and chronic pain
  • Inflammation and swelling
  • Sports injuries and recovery
  • Joint and muscle discomfort
  • Nerve-related pain
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Stress-related tension
  • Postural and movement imbalances

ELECTRO-ACUPUNCTURE

Electro-Acupuncture is an advanced form of acupuncture that combines traditional needle placement with gentle electrical stimulation. This technique enhances the therapeutic effects of acupuncture by providing a steady, rhythmic pulse to targeted points, helping the body release tension, reduce pain, and restore healthy nerve and muscle function. The stimulation is mild and carefully adjusted to your comfort level, creating a deeply relaxing and effective treatment experience.

What Electro-Acupuncture Can Treat

  • Chronic and acute pain
  • Nerve pain and sciatica
  • Muscle spasms and tightness
  • Joint discomfort
  • Sports injuries
  • Inflammation
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Recovery after surgery or injury
